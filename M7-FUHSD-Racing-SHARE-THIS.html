M7 History

The M7 legacy began with a vision to create a racing team that embodies precision, innovation, and relentless pursuit of excellence. Founded by passionate motorsport enthusiasts from the Southern Bay Area, M7 represents more than just a team‚Äîit's a movement.

The M7 designation pays homage to legendary racing heritage while forging a new path in modern motorsport. Our team combines cutting-edge technology with traditional racing values to create a unique identity in the competitive racing world.

FUHSD Racing History

FUHSD Racing emerged from the Fremont Union High School District, bringing together talented drivers and passionate supporters who share a common love for motorsport. What started as a grassroots initiative has evolved into a competitive racing operation.

Our journey through various racing series has taught us invaluable lessons about teamwork, dedication, and the pursuit of perfection. Each race, each challenge, and each victory has shaped us into the team we are today.

Polytrack

Polytrack represents our commitment to diverse racing disciplines. From karting to endurance racing, we compete across multiple platforms to sharpen our skills and test our limits.

This multi-series approach ensures our drivers are well-rounded competitors capable of adapting to any racing environment, from the tight corners of K1 Santa Clara to the high-speed straights of international circuits.
